About Me:

My name is Carter Johnson, I grew up in Salt Lake City for the majority of my life, and have been incredibly blessed to call Utah my home. I'm currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp through the University of Utah, and it has been the best decision I've ever made. I'm working towards becoming a full-stack web developer and can't wait to see where this path takes me.
